Pain-Free Dental Services

Experience our gentle approach to dentistry with advanced pain management techniques and sedation options for a comfortable, anxiety-free visit.

Dental Scaling & Polishing

It is a simple, painless, non-surgical procedure simply called teeth cleaning in which we remove the(yellow brown deposits) calculus and stains that are present on the tooth surface using an ultrasonic scaler followed by polishing using polishing paste. This treatment process helps in restoring gum health and thus better oral hygiene.

Root Canal Treatment

It is a treatment recommended to remove the inflamed /infected pulp using local anesthesia, clean and disinfect the root canal, followed by filling & sealing to prevent reinfection of the tooth. RCT treated tooth should be covered with a cap (crown) to protect it from oral environment.

Dental Implants

It is the best treatment option for replacing the missing teeth. It is a surgical procedure in which a titanium implant is placed in the bone of the missing teeth or damaged tooth that cannot be preserved with RCT. Dental implants acts as an artificial root upon which the dental crown (cap) is placed.

Dental Extractions

It is a process of removing the severely damaged/ broken tooth, root pieces, retained primary tooth, and extra teeth using local anesthesia. preventing further infection and pain caused due to infection. Permanent teeth except wisdom should be replaced after extraction.

Wisdom Teeth Complaints

These are the last teeth to erupt in the oral cavity hence may not have enough space resulting in impaction. Improperly erupted wisdom teeth may cause pain, swollen gums, food lodgment, tooth decay, damage to adjacent teeth, jaw pain etc. good to remove such teeth to get relief from pain and prevent damage to adjacent teeth.

Dental Braces

Dental Braces are used for the correction of irregularly placed, crowded teeth, gaps between teeth, forwardly placed teeth etc.. using metal or ceramic or self-ligating braces etc Patient need to visit the clinic to get the treatment from the orthodontist once in every month.

Invisible Aligners

Invisible aligners are extremely less noticeable thin plastic shells, custom made to fit your teeth as you wear them apply gentle and contact forces on teeth to align irregularly placed teeth for all age groups depending on the bone strength. Our doctors will take your opg x-ray to understand the strength of your bone and can arrange a treatment plan

Dental OPG X-Ray

OPGs (Orthopantograms) are a type of dental x-ray that produces a wide 2-dimensional dental x-ray of upper and lower teeth and adjacent structures like bone sinuses etc. OPGs are used in routine dental checkups check the oral health.

Teeth Whitening

It is a process that lightens the color of the teeth using chemical agents. This process helps to remove both internal and external stains and helps to improve the shade of the teeth.

Cavity Fillings

It is a process in which a decayed part of the teeth is removed, then fills the teeth with filling materials. Cavities not involving pulp (painless) of the tooth can be treated by fillings. It is better to do a filling in case of small cavities to prevent food lodgment and further damage to teeth.

Lost Tooth Replacement Bridges

In case of a missing tooth, it is better to replace as early as possible to avoid drifting of lateral and opposing arch teeth. If a or multiple teeth are missing, with the support of adjacent teeth, missing teeth can be replaced, this is called dental bridge. Recommended if implants are not indicated.

Gum Surgeries

Gum surgeries address various periodontal issues, such as gum recession, gum disease, and tissue regeneration. Procedures range from gum grafts to flap surgery, aiming to restore oral health and aesthetics.

Broken Teeth Management

Depending on the amount of loss of tooth structure, broken teeth can be managed with composite filling (less than 3mm fracture) veneers or crowns in case of more loss. Broken piece can also be fixed if the lost part is intact.

Pediatric Dentistry

It includes all the treatments done in children like dental fillings, pulp therapy if the cavity is too big, extraction, cleft lip and cleft palate surgeries, extraction of teeth present at the time of birth (natal neonatal teeth).

Dental Laser Treatment

Dental lasers are used for soft tissue surgeries, RCT, hypersensitivity, teeth whitening, disinfection and stimulation of soft tissues after surgical procedures.
