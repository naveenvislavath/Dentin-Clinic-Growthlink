Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading dental care provider in Hyderabad, our journey has been defined by a commitment to excellence and innovation in dental care.

The Beginning of Excellence

Founded in 2010 by Dr. Suryanarayan Sharma, Dentino Oral Experts began with a vision to revolutionize dental care in Hyderabad. Dr. Sharma, having completed his advanced training in pain-free dentistry techniques in the United States, returned to India with a mission to make dental care comfortable, accessible, and anxiety-free for all patients.

What started as a small clinic with just three dental chairs has now grown into a state-of-the-art facility with multiple specialized departments, cutting-edge technology, and a team of over 50 dental professionals dedicated to providing exceptional care.

Our growth has been guided by our unwavering commitment to patient comfort and satisfaction. We pioneered the concept of pain-free dentistry in Hyderabad, introducing advanced sedation techniques and gentle procedures that have transformed the dental experience for thousands of patients who previously feared dental visits.

Our Pain-Free Approach

Discover how we've revolutionized dental care by making comfort and anxiety management central to our treatment philosophy.

The Science of Comfort

At Dentino Oral Experts, we've developed a comprehensive approach to pain-free dentistry that addresses both physical discomfort and psychological anxiety. Our methodology combines advanced technology, specialized techniques, and personalized care to ensure a comfortable experience for every patient.

Advanced Local Anesthesia

We use computer-controlled delivery systems and buffered anesthetics for painless injections and more effective numbing.

Sedation Options

From mild nitrous oxide to oral sedation and IV sedation, we offer multiple options tailored to your anxiety level and procedure complexity.

Minimally Invasive Techniques

Laser dentistry, air abrasion, and other modern approaches that reduce or eliminate drilling, cutting, and discomfort.

Creating a Comfort-First Environment

Beyond clinical techniques, we've designed every aspect of our practice to reduce anxiety and enhance comfort. From the moment you enter our clinic, you'll experience a different kind of dental visit.

Calming Environment

Soothing decor, aromatherapy, and noise-cancelling headphones create a peaceful atmosphere that helps reduce anxiety.

Distraction Techniques

Ceiling-mounted TVs, virtual reality headsets, and music options help take your mind off the procedure.

Communication & Control

We establish clear signals so you can pause treatment at any time, giving you a sense of control throughout your visit.
